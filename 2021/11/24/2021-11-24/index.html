DGL03:MY FIRST ENEMY!!!

Posted on 2021-11-24 Views: Views: Valine:

≧◉◡◉≦

HI~ It’s been a long time…

I’ve been learning the UNITY for the last month and a half, so there was no chance to promote the development process for me.

But I’m happy to tell you now…

I finally created something!

Now, it’s time for the children’s favorite part:

▽ SHOWCASE ▽

☸ENEMY_SNAKE:

​ The enemy is a very important existence.

​ I think their varied attack patterns are part of the game’s entertainment.

​ In fact,they are half-baked.

​ Because,I didn’t give sprites for them…

​ They are not beautiful…but practical ●▽● ~

​ After all , I spent a lot of time writing the code on them.

​ Hey! Yeah, they look just like me! hh

​ I plan to make their skins late in the dev, then you will see some substitutes in the future dev-blog.

​ Those appearances may not be them.

​ ✎Talk about the first enemy~

TYPE NAME BIRTHPLACE Level ATTACK PATTERN
Enemy Snake 1Black Snake Dungeon 1 Impact

​ If attacked, they will bounce off like balls..

​ That is funny.＞▽＜

​ When they lose a lot of blood , they don’t just disappear. There may be a way out of your sight, but it’s not death. I don’t want such despair to exist in this world.

​ But if it does exist, it must be their own choice.

​ He’s just a simple little snake~

​ Let me celebrate his birth~~~\﻿ (•◡•) /

☸PLANT_REED:

​ That’s really a big problem for me. Although it was been solved now, it bothered me at the time.

​

​ As you can see, those reeds are so springy that I can fly to another place.

​ If you are not strong enough, you may fall in the middle.

​ That is too dangerous!

​ So even pulling the reeds became very stressful!

​ C’est la vie

​ ✎Talk about the reeds~

TYPE NAME BIRTHPLACE FUNCTION
Plant Reed 1Black Snake Dungeon Leap

​ It took my four whole days. ＞﹏＜

​ I really learned a lot when I implemented this feature.

​ The code and the engine, of course.

​ And…..patience.

​ Unfortunately， I couldn’t do it in a more vibrant way. Now, it’s more like two completely unrelated things doing their own thing.

​ In the future study and dev, I will pay more attention to these and make them look really alive.

There are also some new elements, such as the UI , lotus and stones……

<‘^′>

The next time when I come to update the blog.

I will be bringing new monster friends, new plants, and of course all the necessary weapons for a hero!

Expect that day to come in 2021. ٩(●̮̃•)۶

Be happy~
